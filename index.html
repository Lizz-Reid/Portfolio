Hello,

I'm Violet

Design has always been my passion, but finding the perfect fit has been a journey. In a graphic and product design course, I discovered joy in the design process and psychology.

I then detoured into architecture which still did not feel right, yet it revealed my love for working within design briefs. This exploration naturally led me to the exciting world of UI/UX design, where all my design interests seamlessly come together. I am grateful for my previous design opportunities that gave me a well-rounded approach to design.

UX Skills

User Research

After conducting user interviews, I compile a survey highlighting the main insights gleaned from the interviews.

This helps me delve deeper into the insights before moving on to the ideation phase.

.

User Empathy

I start by crafting a proto persona to pinpoint the presumed target audience and their goals.

After conducting interviews, I revisit and refine the target audience and their goals using the real-world data I've gathered.

Qualitative Interviews

I identify my main objectives of the interviews, formulate questions aligned with each objective and meticulously assess each question to ensure it will elicit valuable data.
